Serve'72

Serve is a local volleybalclub. Since there website was old and not up to date they needed a new one.

The design is done by Pluk Ontwerpbureau . I transformed the designs to a real usable product.

This website uses MODX as CMS. This CMS is pretty cool since it I have all the freedom there is. Everything is possible and it's easily expandable by building your own snippets. MODX is also very easy to use for content changes.

This website is still being used. Have a look at: serve72.nl .
